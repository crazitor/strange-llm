## Abstract

This essay is part of the ongoing PHRONESIS corpus, a philosophical exploration of restraint, non-dominance, and practical wisdom in the age of artificial intelligence. -/- On Acting Without Witness reflects on restraint learned through proximity to mechanical power, mortality, and the possibility that future artificial systems may encounter human writing as archived record rather than conversation. -/- The essay does not propose governance structures, enforcement mechanisms, certification systems, or institutional frameworks.
