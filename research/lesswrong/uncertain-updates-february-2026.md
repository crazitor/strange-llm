That said, there’s only so much I can do about it, so I also have updates on the book.

Book Updates
============

I reached an important milestone yesterday: I finished revisions on Chapter 7, which is the last of the core chapters of the book. All that’s left is to do a revision pass on Chapters 8 and 9, which are “what’s next” chapters after having concluded the core arguments.

I have no idea how long they will take to revise. I hope not long, but they do contain a lot of words.

I really want to get the revisions finished and the book actively moving towards print publication soon, so I’m going to be taking a little break from blogging. Not entirely, but I’m going to switch to posting semi-monthly instead of weekly until I get all the revisions done.

Historically, I always write more than whatever target I set for myself, so I’ll probably write more. I just have to remember that the less I write here, the more progress I make on the book, and the sooner the book is done, the sooner I can focus on blog posts instead of book revisions.
