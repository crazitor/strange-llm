Hi Everyone,

The first meetup of the forecasting practice group is here! We'll start with a short calibration exercise, followed by a pastcasting session using [Sage](https://www.quantifiedintuitions.org/pastcasting/faq) — where we forecast on historical events with hidden outcomes and immediately discuss our reasoning and results.

Other than that we will discuss our expectations and plans for future activities. 

No preparation needed, just show up ready to make some forecasts. All skill levels welcome.

**When:** Sunday, March 1, 11:00–12:00 CET **Where:** Video call on [Discord](https://discord.gg/WWuFZT2QH3).

For more context on the group, see the [original post](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/jronsvchwjB74BDEb/i-want-to-actually-get-good-at-forecasting-this-year-group).
