Two years ago, I posted detailed [lecture notes](https://scottaaronson.blog/?p=3943) on this blog for my Intro to Quantum Information Science undergrad course at UT Austin. Today, with enormous thanks to UT PhD student Corey Ostrove, we've gotten the notes into a much better shape (for starters, they're now in LaTeX). You can [see the results here](https://www.scottaaronson.com/qclec.pdf) (7MB)--it's basically a 260-page introductory quantum computing textbook in beta form, covering similar material as many other introductory quantum computing textbooks, but in my style for those who like that. It's missing exercises, as well as material on quantum supremacy experiments, recent progress in hardware, etc., but that will be added in the next version if there's enough interest. Enjoy!
