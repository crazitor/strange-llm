**A few comments:**

  1. It's (just-barely) conceivable that the results could have been _slightly_ skewed by the quantum- and complexity-loving readership of this blog.
  2. Voters _really_ didn't like fiction/pop-culture references, mechanical contrivances, or anything that sounded like a publicity stunt. They were much keener on conceptual advances (even to the extent of putting Gödel well ahead of the transistor).
