Thanks to all the readers who've stuck with me to this point, to the students for a fantastic semester (if they still remember it) as well as their scribing help, to Chris Granade for further scribing, and to Waterloo's Institute for Quantum Computing for letting me get away with this. I hope you've enjoyed it, and only wish I'd kept my end of the bargain by getting these notes done a year earlier.
