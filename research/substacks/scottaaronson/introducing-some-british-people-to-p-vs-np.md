For readers of this blog, there won't be anything new here, but, well … you might enjoy [the rest of the hour-long programme [sic]](http://www.thenakedscientists.com/HTML/podcasts/naked-scientists/show/20150721/), which also includes segments about a few other Clay Millennium problems (the Riemann Hypothesis, Navier-Stokes, and Poincaré), as well as a segment about fat fish that live in caves and gorge themselves on food on the rare occasions when it becomes available, and which turn out to share a gene variant with humans with similar tendencies.
