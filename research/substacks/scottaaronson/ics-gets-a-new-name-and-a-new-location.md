Shafi Goldwasser has asked me to announce that the next Innovations in Theoretical Computer Science (ITCS) conference--previously called Innovations in Computer Science (ICS)--will be held January 8-10, 2012 in Cambridge, MA, the first I(T)CS to be held outside of China.   The submission deadline is August 7.  The call for papers is [here](http://projects.csail.mit.edu/itcs2012/), and the conference website is [here]( http://research.microsoft.com/itcs2012/).
