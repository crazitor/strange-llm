On that note, please enjoy my new [one-hour podcast on Spotify](https://nanotronics.co/thinkspace/24-scott-aaronson-before-and-after-the-machine/) (if that link doesn’t work, [try this one](https://overcast.fm/+UZFvS_CT8)) with [Matthew Putman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matthew_Putman_\(scientist\)) of Utility+. Alas, my umming and ahhing were more frequent than I now aim for, but that's partly compensated for by Matthew's excellent decision to speed up the audio. This was an unusually wide-ranging interview, covering everything from SlateStarCodex to quantum gravity to interdisciplinary conferences to the challenges of teaching quantum computing to 7-year-olds. I hope you like it!
