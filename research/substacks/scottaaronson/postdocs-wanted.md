[David Soloveichik](https://www.solo-group.link/), my friend and colleague in UT Austin's Electrical and Computer Engineering department, and I are looking to hire a joint postdoc in "Unconventional Computing," broadly defined. Areas of interest include but are not limited to:

(1) quantum computation,  
(2) thermodynamics of computation and reversible computation,  
(3) analog computation, and  
(4) chemical computation.

The ideal candidate would have broad multi-disciplinary interests in addition to prior experience and publications in at least one of these areas. The researcher will work closely with David and myself but is expected to be highly self-motivated. To apply, please send an email to [david.soloveichik@utexas.edu](mailto:david.soloveichik@utexas.edu) and [aaronson@cs.utexas.edu](mailto:aaronson@cs.utexas.edu) with the subject line "quantum postdoc application." Please include a CV and links to three representative publications. Let's set a deadline of January 20th. We'll be back in touch if we need recommendation letters.
