Has the famous Turing Test been passed? 

The headlines seem to say so. 

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!py9i!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4fa37e16-8495-4038-85ca-73d988ad592b_1404x966.png)

But are these recent “Turing Tests” at all what Alan Turing had in mind with his “imitation game”? 

For _Science_ _Magazine_ I wrote about the redefinition of the Turing Test and how the history of AI has spurred a continual shifting in our conceptualizations of what intelligence is. 

I hope you will check it out, and let me know your thoughts. <https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.adq9356>
