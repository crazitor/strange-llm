A brief update:

  1. Some battles no longer need fighting. The notion [on which this Substack was founded in May 2022 ](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/the-new-science-of-alt-intelligence) — that LLM scaling would not bring us to AGI — has gone mainstream.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sWrq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F36983697-f6ef-4346-b7f4-038802e423d1_960x1481.png)

Even Palantir’s CEO Alex Karp, has apparently gone full Gary Marcus:

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EjNF!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F319cd510-fd65-4ae7-b396-ef9ecd531acc_1294x1062.png)

  2. Perhaps I also no longer need to warn people that the idea that coding would disappear soon is bullshit. Anyone remember this prediction from Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei back in March? Some journalists I could name seem to take it seriously at the time. (I didn’t, instead calling it [fantasy](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/ai-coding-fantasy-meets-pac-man?utm_source=publication-search).)

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iEIZ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1d7f20b7-fd46-47b4-b296-b6afdaa1126f_1363x1361.webp)

It was. Even the generally pro industry _The Information_ now sees that:

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5xmv!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa47f7059-fba3-4501-b35d-412ed70aa6a3_563x540.png)

  3. The concerns about potential copyright violations that Reid Southen and I expressed here in and at greater length [in the IEEE Spectrum in January 2024](https://spectrum.ieee.org/midjourney-copyright) continue almost two years later to be a problem.

Disney cited our work extensively in [their June lawsuit](https://www.courtlistener.com/docket/70513159/disney-enterprises-inc-v-midjourney-inc/), premised on exactly our concerns. Yesterday Warner filed a similar suit (also drawing heavily on my work with Southen). 

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9a9W!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0d0715d-6d62-4d96-8a70-4e08cb13f024_1206x2196.jpeg)

As far as I know, the problem of wildly derivative outputs has not gone away. (All the replies we got on Twitter at the time about how the problem could be easily rectified were, as usual, nonsense.)

  4. Meanwhile, more darkly, [the Jurassic Park moment of mass-produced AI-generated misinformation that I projected in December 2022](https://open.substack.com/pub/garymarcus/p/ais-jurassic-park-moment?r=8tdk6&utm_campaign=post&utm_medium=web&showWelcomeOnShare=false) has arrived.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!uc5u!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb6e2f528-b22e-46fc-a008-5b09e2a844c7_961x922.png)

If there is any true exponential nowadays, I fear it is this: the rapid doubling of AI-generated misinformation.

  5. Sad also to say that my [December 2022 predictions of deaths by chatbot](https://www.wired.com/story/large-language-models-artificial-intelligence/) have now [also been confirmed](https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2025/08/chatgpt-helped-teen-plan-suicide-after-safeguards-failed-openai-admits/), [more than once.](https://www.wsj.com/tech/ai/chatgpt-ai-stein-erik-soelberg-murder-suicide-6b67dbfb) (Then there is also the correlated issue of AI-induced delusions, which I did not foresee.)

  6. My darkest projections, though, were about the rise of techno-fascism. You can [hear Garry Kasparov and I discuss them in a podcast just dropped today](https://www.theatlantic.com/podcasts/archive/2025/09/ai-and-the-fight-between-democracy-and-autocracy/684095/?preview=pINTKDhyR-2jUqa0s4M1fNG54mg). (There is also a transcript at the same link).

Let us all hope that we can learn something from the mistakes of the last few years, and find a new path — to an AI that better serves humanity.
