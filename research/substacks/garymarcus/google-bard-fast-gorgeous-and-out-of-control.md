Looks like Google’s [brand-new Bard image generation software](https://www.theverge.com/2024/2/1/24057438/bard-gemini-imagen-google-ai-image-generation) is going to keep even more IP lawyers gainfully employed this year…

Not every image looks like this, but it’s pretty clear after a few minutes experimentation from a friend that (a) Bard makes fantastic images but that (b) it is drawing from copyrighted sources and (c)[ as capable as the other systems Reid Southen and I looked at](https://spectrum.ieee.org/midjourney-copyright) in returning derivative artwork that appears to infringe without attribution.
