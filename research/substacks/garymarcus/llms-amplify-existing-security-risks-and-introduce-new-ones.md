Easily jailbroken AI agents that excel at mimicry but lack the capacity and imperative to evaluate the consequences of their actions are going to cause massive harm.

[“Mainlining them” into the veins of society](https://news.sky.com/story/uk-to-mainline-ai-in-the-veins-under-new-plans-from-sir-keir-starmer-13287743), as the UK proposes to do, is a huge mistake.
