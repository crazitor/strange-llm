In a [recent essay](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/the-great-ai-retrenchment-has-begun) (which I have now updated), I misunderstood Ray Kurzweil to be revising his prediction for AGI to a later year (perhaps 2032), based on this except from a recent [interview](https://www.wired.com/story/big-interview-ray-kurzweil/?bxid=5c748a4d3f92a46844a21302&bxid=5c748a4d3f92a46844a21302&cndid=52818373&cndid=52818373&esrc=bounceX&esrc=bounceX&hasha=a14a422457611d33e19642a97e42a61d&hasha=a14a422457611d33e19642a97e42a61d&hashb=102d8032a4c4a4d63aaac79bcbf6a1eedd8598c8&hashb=102d8032a4c4a4d63aaac79bcbf6a1eedd8598c8&hashc=463cb4a391f6533f86d813b64de48b6db1d35d9310a0a6969e5bff80b806c34f&hashc=463cb4a391f6533f86d813b64de48b6db1d35d9310a0a6969e5bff80b806c34f&source=Email_0_EDT_WIR_NEWSLETTER_0_DAILY_ZZ&source=Email_0_EDT_WIR_NEWSLETTER_0_DAILY_ZZ&utm_brand=wired&utm_brand=wired&utm_campaign=aud-dev&utm_campaign=aud-dev&utm_content=WIR_Daily_061324&utm_content=WIR_Daily_061324&utm_mailing=WIR_Daily_061324&utm_mailing=WIR_Daily_061324&utm_medium=email&utm_medium=email&utm_source=nl&utm_source=nl&utm_term=WIR_Daily_Active&utm_term=WIR_Daily_Active):

Ray just emailed me (22 June 2024) with an important clarification of his position:

> _I have not revised and not redefined my prediction of AGI, still defined as AI that can perform any cognitive task an educated human can. I still still believe that will happen by 2029. My comment to Steven Levy was intended to specify that reaching the level of the best human poets is a higher bar for writing than what a "mere" AGI would achieve, and thus might take longer._

Kudos to him for staying strong to his position.
