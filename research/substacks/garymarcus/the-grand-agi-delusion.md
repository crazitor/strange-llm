We aren’t close to AGI, but we are reshaping our world on the premise that we are. 

I think we are making a huge mistake.

My 25 minute talk about this, delivered at the Royal Society on October 2, on the occasion of the 75th Anniversary of Turing Test can be found [here](https://www.youtube.com/live/GmnBTCKocZI?t=5977). [Time stamp: 1:39.37]

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!T3Qh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6ea78daf-c2e6-4339-89af-91c59dd314f6_1536x864.jpeg)[The Grand AGI delusion](https://www.youtube.com/live/GmnBTCKocZI?t=5977)

“But, wait, there’s more”, as late night TV guy Ron Popeil used to say. The legendary inventor Alan Kay opened the same event with a brilliant and personal talk on [hubris, power, technology, and black swan events that could affect us all](https://www.youtube.com/live/GmnBTCKocZI?t=1012): [Time stamp: 0:16.52]

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!L0IC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc63d6ed7-f8e0-461f-a406-9ccaff384f35_1642x956.png)[Alan Kay’s warning to us all](https://www.youtube.com/live/GmnBTCKocZI?t=1012)

And, in a life moment that I will never forget, here is the legendary Peter Gabriel introducing me [and distilling into a few minutes and a few pointed questions so much of what is so important about our moment in history](https://www.youtube.com/live/GmnBTCKocZI?t=5735s). [Time stamp: 1:35.35]

Watch, for example, for the eviscerating remarks on contemporary AI from Shannon Vallor, a hilarious live reenactment of the absurd variety of tests in the Turing’s original paper, and trenchant closing remarks from Alan Turing’s nephew Dermot Turing.
