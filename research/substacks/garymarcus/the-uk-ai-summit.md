Lost in my email was a very important, perhaps historic [open letter ](https://www.openrightsgroup.org/press-releases/letter-to-rishi-sunak-ai-summit/) (AI SUMMIT IS DOMINATED BY BIG TECH AND A ‘MISSED OPPORTUNITY’) that I have now signed. It was drafted by [Connected by Data](https://connectedbydata.org/), [The Trades Union Congress](https://www.tuc.org.uk/) and [Open Rights Group](https://www.openrightsgroup.org/). and signed by Mozilla, Amnesty International, the AFL-CIO, the ETUC and [many more](https://ai-summit-open-letter.info/). I see this as landmark moment.
