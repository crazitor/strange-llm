And by the way, no need to worry, says OpenAI, because our models can _reason_ :

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jABA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc4b2450a-6f91-4a1f-b56e-a6d47c8982e6_1188x310.png)[Interview with ABC News, March 16](https://abcnews.go.com/Technology/openai-ceo-sam-altman-ai-reshape-society-acknowledges/story?id=97897122) , mischaracterizing what GPT does

True LLMs don’t (just) memorize, and true that their models make for lousy databases (for a database a hallucination is an outright fail) but if the definition of _reasoning_ is to obtain valid conclusions from known facts, GPT-4 frequently falls short there, too.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c0IQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faefa4be2-26dd-4278-bcb1-77148e231663_1242x1155.jpeg)
