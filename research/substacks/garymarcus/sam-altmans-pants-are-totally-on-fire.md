The fact that Sam Altman is a liar is no longer news. As I argued here in [late 2023](https://open.substack.com/pub/garymarcus/p/not-consistently-candid?r=8tdk6&utm_campaign=post&utm_medium=web&showWelcomeOnShare=false), Altman truly was fired for being “not completely candid” — just like the board said. Recent books by Karen Hao and Keach Hagey pretty much confirm this. I dissected his 2023 Senate testimony [here](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/article/2024/aug/03/open-ai-sam-altman-chatgpt-gary-marcus-taming-silicon-valley).

But just in case any one was seriously still in doubt a just-released [62-page deposition](https://storage.courtlistener.com/recap/gov.uscourts.cand.433688/gov.uscourts.cand.433688.340.1.pdf) from Ilya Sutskever ought to seal the deal:

The kicker is this: Sam was, once again, lying his ass off. What he meant by “we do not have or want government guarantees for OpenAI data centers” was actually that … OpenAI explicitly [asked the White House office of science and technology (OSTP) to consider Federal loan guarantees, just a week earlier](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/21b88bb5-10a3-4566-919d-f9a6b9c3e632/openai-ostp-rfi-oct-27-2025.pdf):

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!1yhA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F025b4f84-65dc-4746-aba1-7c4dbe64ba7f_1129x1313.jpeg)

In a [podcast that was probably recorded in last several days](https://x.com/moreperfectus/status/1986493064064671904?s=61), Altman also appears to have been laying the groundwork for loan guarantees:

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-YFR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3528a16-a84f-4f53-9177-e7053a44a3d4_1322x1267.png)

In short, Altman was—likely in conjunction with Nvidia, [which also just seemed to be laying groundwork for a bailout](https://x.com/ross__hendricks/status/1986192499598143750?s=61)—launching a full court press for loan guarantees when he got caught with hands in the cookie jar. 

And then Altman lied about the whole thing to the entire world. Even David Sacks at the White House may have been conned.

Nobody should _ever_ trust this man. Ever.
