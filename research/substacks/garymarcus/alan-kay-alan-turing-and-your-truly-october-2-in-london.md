_I don’t usually announce events here but this one is special:_

On October 2, in celebration of the 75th Anniversary of the Turing Test, [the legendary Alan Kay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alan_Kay), one of the greatest inventors and thinkers of our time (and also a loyal supporter of this Substack) speaks at The Royal Society.

Bonus and full disclosure, I am on the agenda, too. 

A few tickets have just been released to the public, details [here](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/celebrating-the-75th-anniversary-of-the-turing-test-tickets-1512110997699?aff=oddtdtcreator).
